About me

Hello! My name is Alejandro Romero, but i often go by Alex. I am currently studying Computer Science at Oregon State University and expecting to graduate March 2018. Having received my first degree in Linguistics at University of Florida, I was afforded the unique opportunity to live work and study abroad in various countries including Japa, Russia, and Costa Rica. Having worked as a language educator for quite some time, I began to imagine myself one day created the next big language learning application. This eventually lead me pursue my current post-bacc degree.


Apart from studying, i enjoy working on side projects and expanding my knowledge of various new programming languages and technologies. I have made a few apps including an old-school "Adventure" type game and a note taking android app amongst others which can be found on my portfolio page. When i'm not dabbling with programs you can often find me playing board games with my friends. I am talking more like Settlers of Catan and Dominion as opposed to Monopoly and Clue, but those are fun too.
